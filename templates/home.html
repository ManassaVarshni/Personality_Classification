Warm welcome to my project on personalities!

This project is based on the famous "Big Five" personality dataset. To find out your personality all you have to do is click on the Test button above and spend about 5-8 minutes to answer a set of questions!!.

About

Project Details

Have you ever wondered what type of personality you have? Or how much of an extroverted person you are?


I have done exactly this in this project. Using the Big Five Personality dataset, and with the help of Machine Learning algorithms, I have developed a project which will tell you your personality scores across five broad types - extroversion, agreeableness, openness, conscientiousness, and neuroticism.


All you have to do is click on the Test button and answer some of the questions asked. Sit back and wait for your results to appear!
